MY FAVOURITE MALAYALAM MOVIES

Top 5 old malayalam moview you must watch

1.YAKSHI

A movie based on malayattoor's novel in the name Yakshi. The movie staring satyan and sharada portray's the need of scientific thinking and points towards how superstitions affects humans. The major positives are the realistic acting and the timeless story.

2.Anubhavangal Paalichakal

A movie which shows the grey shade of human relations . How marital relations get disrupted and love remains even after breakdown of a relation. The movie shows a progressive viewpoint about extra marital relations and is far ahead of its time.

3.Yavanika

A K G George thriller which has signature of the legendary director. It starts with disappearing of a teenager and eventual finding of her corpse.The movie unweils with investigation progressing.

4.Thoovanathumbikal

What image does the word prostituition bring to your mind? A helpless feeble woman? The movie tells the story of Clara a woman who takes up the profession.Of Unnikrishnan a patriarchial male who enters her life how their relation evolves and more.

5.Thaniyavarthanam

Mental illness is one of the most stereotyped in our society. The movie shows how society,superstitions and family pushes a man into a vicious cycle which makes him branded as mad. The story clearly asks "Who is lunactic?the society or the protagonist"
